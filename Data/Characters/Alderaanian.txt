They were also careful to live in harmony with their [[planet]], which was renowned for its untarnished beauty.<ref name=\"Leia 2\">[[Princess Leia 2|''Princess Leia'' 2]]</ref>\n\nSome Alderaanian [[Gender|males]], like [[Nash Windrider]], and [[Bail Prestor Organa/Legends|Bail Prestor Organa]]{{Fact}} wore their [[hair]] long by tradition.<ref name=\"Lost Stars\">''[[Lost Stars]]''</ref>\n\n==History==\nEarly in their history, the Alderaanians employed [[soldier]]s like most other cultures, before gradually developing into a pacifist society. Over [[time]], Alderaanians became noted for sharing their knowledge with the rest of the [[Galactic community]].<ref name=\"Leia 2\" />\n\nThroughout history, a number of Alderaanians emigrated to other planets, founding communities and exporting their unique culture far from their [[homeworld]]. Such offworld communities notably existed on [[Naboo]]<ref name=\"Leia 2\" /> and [[Sullust]].<ref name=\"Leia 3\">[[Princess Leia 3|''Princess Leia'' 3]]</ref> Generations before the [[Galactic Civil War]], a number of Alderaanians also established their presence on the planet of [[Espirion]], which was known for being open to [[refugee]]s and wanderers. There, they intermarried with the [[Espirion (species)|natives]], a [[species]] of [[Color|red]]-skinned [[humanoid]]s with tapered [[ear]]s. As a result, a population of [[Alder-Espirion|half-Alderaanians]], exhibiting non-human characteristics, arose on Espirion. In the immediate aftermath of the [[Battle of Yavin]], their community was led by [[Chief]] [[Beon Beonel]].<ref name=\"Leia 4\" />\n\nWhen Alderaan was [[The Disaster|destroyed]] by the [[Galactic Empire]] to test the capabilities of their new [[DS-1 Death Star Mobile Battle Station|Death Star]] [[battle station]], most Alderaanians perished along with their home planet. Some of them, however, were offworld at the time of the planet's destruction. Shortly after the Battle of [[Yavin Prime|Yavin]], during which the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic|Rebel Alliance]] retaliated against the Empire and destroyed the Death Star, [[Prince]]ss [[Leia Skywalker Organa Solo|Leia Organa]] set off on a [[Mission to rescue the survivors of Alderaan|mission]] to rescue the surviving sons and daughters of Alderaan.<ref name=\"Leia 1\" />\n\nSome Alderaanians who served in the Alliance to Restore the Republic wore a tattoo of the [[Rebel Alliance starbird]] beneath their left eye to symbolise the planet they came from and the people they lost.<ref>''[[Chapter 12: The Siege]]''</ref> This became widespread enough that Imperial veterans were able to recognise the tattoo on sight and know that the wearer came from Alderaan, as was the
