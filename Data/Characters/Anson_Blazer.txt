He and his partners were identified by anti-Rebel [[Kiran Tatch]], who had not crossed paths with them before. When Tatch reported his findings to the Kalstans the operation had to be cut with Blazer and his partners being chased by the [[Pirate/Legends|pirates]] to a great cost in equipment. In that functionality he wrote extensive reports on potential threats for the Rebel Alliance such as old acquaintance Kiran Tatch and Dr. [[Sere Lure]]. Blazer specified that Lure was a threat for aquatic Rebel units but that those same aquatic units might themselves spy on Lure.
