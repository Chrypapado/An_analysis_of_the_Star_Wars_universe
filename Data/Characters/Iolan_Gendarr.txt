The Star Destroyer ''Reliance'' led attacks on New Republic systems, utilizing [[Scimitar assault bomber]]s in concert with [[Walker/Legends|walker]] ground assaults. He had a receding hairline and a thin beard that hugged his jawline. A scar ran down the right side of his face from his forehead to his jaw, narrowly missing his [[Eye/Legends|eye]] and bisecting his eyebrow. Gendarr was a competent naval commander and tactician, with specialities in [[Capital ship/Legends|capital ship]] and [[Starfighter/Legends|starfighter]] strategies. He was also an intimidating and persuasive individual.<ref name=\"WBC\" />\n\n==Skills and abilities==\nGendarr was trained to use a [[Blaster/Legends|blaster]], and he had a working knowledge of [[Bureaucrat/Legends|bureaucratic procedure]], along with [[Alien/Legends|alien]] cultures, languages, and [[Star system/Legends|star systems]]. An expert on [[Law enforcement agency/Legends|law enforcement]] procedures, especially in regard to Imperial law, Gendarr could also [[Astrogation/Legends|plot courses]] through [[Hyperspace/Legends|hyperspace]], [[Pilot/Legends|fly]] capital ships, operate their weaponry and perform repairs.
