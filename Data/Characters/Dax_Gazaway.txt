A bustling town, Mos Espa was known for the annual [[Boonta Eve Classic/Legends|Boonta Eve Classic]] [[Podracing/Legends|Podrace]], which attracted fans from across [[The galaxy/Legends|the galaxy]]. By [[24 BBY/Legends|24 BBY]], Gazaway had picked up the profession of Podrace broadcaster and in-race commentator. Gazaway followed the 24 BBY Podracing season, which saw a number of seasoned professionals return to the track alongside a number of newcomers. The season was most notable for the return of the [[Dug/Legends|Dug]] [[Podracer (profession)/Legends|pilot]] [[Sebulba/Legends|Sebulba]], entering the season with a massive [[Collor Pondrat/Legends|Collor Pondrat]] [[Plug-G Gargantua|Plug-G ''Gargantua'' Split-X]] [[Podracer/Legends|Podracer]], and his old nemesis, [[Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Anakin Skywalker]], a former [[Slavery/Legends|slave]] who was by now a [[Jedi/Legends|Jedi]] in training.<ref name=\"Racer Revenge\" />\n\nGazaway followed the competition across the various circuits and courses, broadcasting the races. Gazaway would usually begin the race, previewing some of the course's features before sending the [[Pilot/Legends|pilots]] off. He would then partake in color commentary, remarking on the behavior of certain pilots on the track, as well riffing crashes with numerous metaphors.<ref name=\"Racer Revenge\" /> Despite his occasionally callous remarks, he was said to have had a working relationship with every pilot in the tournament.
