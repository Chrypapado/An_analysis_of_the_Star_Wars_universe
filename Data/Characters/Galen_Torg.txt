By then, he had also joined the Alliance, and continued to use his uniform in the Alliance.<ref name=\"Honor Guard note\" />\n\nIn 0 BBY, the [[Battle of Yavin/Legends|Battle of Yavin]] occurred, during which the Alliance's [[Starfighter/Legends|starfighters]] destroyed the Death Star in the [[Yavin system/Legends|Yavin system]].<ref name=\"NEC\" /> Following the battle, a [[Royal Award Ceremony/Legends|ceremony]] was held in the [[Grand Audience Chamber]] of the [[Great Temple/Legends|Great Temple]], the Alliance's [[Alliance High Command/Legends|headquarters]] on the [[Moon/Legends|moon]] of [[Yavin 4/Legends|Yavin 4]]. Torg was among the [[Soldier/Legends|soldiers]]<ref name=\"ANH\" /> selected<ref name=\"CCG\">{{CCG|set=Premiere Limited|link=Premiere-Light/large/rebelguard.gif|cardname=Rebel Guard}}</ref> to serve as [[Rebel Honor Guard/Legends|Rebel Honor Guard]] sentries for the occasion.
