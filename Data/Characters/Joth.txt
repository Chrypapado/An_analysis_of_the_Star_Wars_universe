When he was interrogated by the spacer, Moril confessed he was hired to deliver animal feed, chemicals and tranquilizer materials to an isolated building and what he saw there terrified him. Lieutenant Joth therefore ordered the spacer to go to the drop location for Moril's shipment and investigate the area.<ref name=\"SWG:CMTDR\">{{SWGcite|exp=SK|type=general|Coronet Murmurs: Tracking Down Rumors|Corellia}}</ref> The spacer quickly discovered that the building was an [[Illegal Lab (Corellia)|illegal lab]],<ref name=\"SWG:CMAS\">{{SWGcite|exp=SK|type=general|Coronet Murmurs: Administrative Secrets|Corellia}}</ref> a lab specialized in [[bio-engineer]]ing creatures through dangerous genetic experiments.
