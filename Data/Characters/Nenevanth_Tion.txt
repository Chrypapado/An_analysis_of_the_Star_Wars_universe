As [[civil war]] broke out between the [[Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]] and the [[Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederacy of Independent Systems]], Tion departed his [[Homeworld/Legends|homeworld]] at the age of fifteen and traveled to [[Coruscant/Legends|Coruscant]], the [[Capital/Legends|capital]] world of the Republic. Shortly after his arrival, Tion was among the first applicants inducted into the [[SAGroup (COMPOR)/Legends|Sub-Adult Group]] (SAGroup), the Republic youth program of the [[Humanocentrism|Human-dominated]] [[Commission for the Protection of the Republic/Legends|Commission for the Protection of the Republic]] (COMPOR).<ref name=\"Insider 70\" />\n\nAs [[Brigade/Legends|brigade]] leader, Tion accompanied six thousand SAGroup brigadiers to a COMPOR rally that was held in the [[Standard year/Legends|year]] [[21 BBY/Legends|21 BBY]] at the [[Monument Plaza/Legends|Monument Plaza]] of [[Galactic City]]. Guarded by two [[Clone trooper/Legends|clone troopers]], Tion delivered a patriotic speech to a large assembly in which he used historical quotes from the [[Kitel Phard Dynasty/Legends|Kitel Phard Dynasty]] in praise of [[Supreme Chancellor/Legends|Supreme Chancellor]] [[Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine's]] leadership of the Republic. Regarding the elimination of corruption and inefficiency that hindered the Republic, Tion quoted an ancient speech made by the [[Emperor/Legends|Emperor]] [[Uueg Tching]] of the [[Atrisian system|Atrisi system]]. Shortly afterward, Chancellor Palpatine addressed the crowd via [[Holocomm/Legends|holocomm]] and commended the loyalty of both COMPOR and its SAGroup chapter. The new autocrat retained the support of COMPOR, itself reformed into the [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order/Legends|Commission for the Preservation of the New Order]] (COMPNOR).
