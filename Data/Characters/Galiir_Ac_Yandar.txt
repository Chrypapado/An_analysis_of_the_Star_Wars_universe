Ekirk deeply despised Galiir but considered that the N.S.H. could be a valuable asset for the Empire. However, the negotiations were disturbed when [[Slicer/Legends|slicers]] of the [[Aggregate Civil Liberties Organization]], an anti-Imperial movement, planted information in Ac Yandar's computer that almost ruined his reputation with the Empire.
