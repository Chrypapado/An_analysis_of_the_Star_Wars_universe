The Rodian claimed it was simply a lucky draw and challenged Revan to more games. He was very proficient at pazaak and took great pride in his skill, often mentioning it to others. He viewed winning as a vindication of this skill. The Rodian was quite bored and welcomed any opportunity to play, as few citizens of Korriban enjoyed the game. However, if someone did not intend to engage in a round with him, he would prefer they leave him alone.
