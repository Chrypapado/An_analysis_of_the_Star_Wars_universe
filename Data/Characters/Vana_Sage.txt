She served for a time with the [[Naboo/Legends|Naboo]] [[Royal Naboo Security Forces/Legends|Royal Security Forces]], but left in disgust after her attempts to get the Naboo to increase their weak defenses were rebuffed. As she grew older, Sage became a mercenary and took a job as a \"troubleshooter\" for the [[Trade Federation of Planets|Trade Federation]], testing the abilities of new Trade Federation equipment. It was around this time that she contracted a rogue [[Xi Char/Legends|Xi Char]] [[Engineer/Legends|Engineer]] to create the ''[[Guardian Mantis]]''. She was also successful in obtaining the [[Bomber/Legends|bomber]] of the [[Feeorin/Legends|Feeorin]] pirate, [[Nym/Legends|Nym]].\n\n[[File:VanaSage.png|thumb|left|Vana Sage, [[Mercenary/Legends|mercenary]]]]\nWhile testing the [[Scarab-class droid starfighter|Scarab]] craft, she intercepted an incriminating transmission sent to the Trade Federation. The federation then attempted to have her terminated. She soon teamed up with [[Bravo Flight/Legends|Bravo Pilot]] [[Rhys Dallows]] and [[Nym/Legends|Nym]] to stop the Federation's invasion of [[Naboo/Legends|Naboo]].\n\nFollowing the battle, Sage accepted a pardon from the [[Galactic Senate/Legends|Galactic Senate]] and a position in the [[Royal Naboo Security Forces/Legends|Royal Naboo Security Forces]]. In [[24 BBY/Legends|24 BBY]], at the request of [[Queen/Legends|Queen]] [[Padm\u00e9 Amidala/Legends|Padm\u00e9 Amidala]], she put together a compilation of information on Nym to be sent to [[Jedi Master/Legends|Jedi Master]] [[Adi Gallia/Legends|Adi Gallia]].\n\n[[22 BBY/Legends|Two years later]], Sage reunited with Nym in time to assist the [[Lok Revenants/Legends|Lok Revenants]] reclaim their base on [[Lok/Legends|Lok]] from [[Trade Federation of Planets|Trade Federation]] forces under [[Cavik Toth]]'s command.
