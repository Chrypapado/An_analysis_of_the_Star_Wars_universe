for the [[2003]] [[Wikipedia:Massively multiplayer online role-playing game|massively multiplayer online role-playing game]] [[Star Wars Galaxies|''Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided'']], including [[Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided Quick Reference Guide|''An Empire Divided Quick Reference Guide'']], the [[Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide|''Total In all of these reference guides, Dervish is listed as being situated on Tatooine.<ref name=\"QuickRef\" /><ref name=\"Total\" /><ref name=\"Complete\">''[[Star Wars Galaxies: The Complete Guide: Prima Official Game Guide]]''</ref> However, it is not clear if Dervish is actually this individual's name, or some kind of title. The ''Quick Reference Guide'' and ''Total Experience'' guide do not capitalize the name,<ref name=\"QuickRef\" /><ref name=\"Total\" /> while the ''Complete Guide'' does.<ref name=\"Complete\" /> The ''Quick Refence Guide'' is also the only one of these three guides to identify Dervish as belonging to a \"social group\" known also as the Dervish.<ref name=\"QuickRef\" /> The other two simply list Dervish as not being affiliated with any particular faction.<ref name=\"Total\" /><ref name=\"Complete\" /> The ''Quick Refence Guide'' identifies Dervish as a level 9 [[Wikipedia:Non-player character|non-player character]],<ref name=\"QuickRef\" /> while the other two identify Dervish as a level 5 NPC.<ref name=\"Total\" /><ref name=\"Complete\" /> The ''Total Experience'' guide further identifies Dervish as an individual skilled as a carbineer and pikeman.<ref name=\"Total\" /> Despite being listed in these reference guides, Dervish is not definitively known to have appeared on the [[video game]]'s live servers as a proper non-player character.<ref name=\"AED\" /> The reference guides do not elaborate on Dervish's [[Species/Legends|species]] or [[Gender/Legends|gender]], nor any quests Dervish may have been associated with.<ref name=\"QuickRef\" /><ref name=\"Total\" /><ref name=\"Complete\" /> Nevertheless, an emulated depiction pulled from this character's Interchange File Format reveals what she would have looked like if she were added to the game. The tree files also contain some information pertaining to camps operated by the Dervish organization.
