Aaaaah!|Palmer's last words|Star Wars: Episode I: Battle for Naboo}}\nPalmer was a [[Gender/Legends|female]] individual born on the peaceful [[Planet/Legends|planet]] of [[Naboo/Legends|Naboo]] and had become an [[Officer/Legends|officer]] in the [[Royal Naboo Security Forces/Legends|Royal Naboo Security Forces]]<ref name=\"Star Wars: Battle for Naboo\" /> by [[32 BBY/Legends|32 BBY]].<ref name=\"The New Essential Chronology\" /> When the [[Trade Federation of Planets|Trade Federation]] [[Invasion of Naboo/Legends|invaded]] her [[Homeworld/Legends|homeworld]], she was captured during the first [[Fall of Theed|stages]] of the invasion. She was later rescued by fellow officer [[Gavyn Sykes]].<ref name=\"The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia\" /> At the [[Time/Legends|time]] of [[Queen/Legends|Queen]] [[Padm\u00e9 Amidala/Legends|Padm\u00e9 Amidala's]] return from [[Coruscant/Legends|Coruscant]]&mdash;the [[Capital/Legends|capital]] planet of the [[Galactic Republic/Legends|Republic]]&mdash;where the Queen was trying to end the invasion in the [[Galactic Senate/Legends|Galactic Senate]], Palmer was stationed at a Naboo [[Naboo Resistance|resistance]] [[Naboo airbase|base]] on the south ridge of the capital [[City/Legends|city]] of Naboo, [[Theed/Legends|Theed]]. The base came under [[Battle of the south ridge|attack]] by elements of the [[Trade Federation Droid Army/Legends|Trade Federation Droid Army]], and she contacted Sykes for assistance. While Sykes was racing to the base, she repeatedly called for reinforcements. Sykes arrived soon after and destroyed the [[Droid/Legends|droid]] attackers.<ref name=\"Star Wars: Battle for Naboo\" />\n\nPalmer later participated in the [[Battle of Naboo/Legends|battle to retake Theed]], leading [[Group 2]], a squad of [[Seraph-class urban landspeeder|Flash Speeders]]. During the battle, Group 3 became pinned down in a plaza, and Palmer was once again saved by Sykes' actions. After the rescue, Palmer and Group 3 helped Sykes escort the [[Naboo commando transport|commando transport]] to the [[Theed Courthouse]]. At the conclusion of the battle, she flew in an [[N-1 starfighter/Legends|N-1 starfighter]] with [[Bravo Flight/Legends|Bravo Flight]] to attack the Trade Federation's [[Vuutun Palaa/Legends|Droid Control Ship]] in [[Orbit/Legends|orbit]] over Naboo, which was controlling the Droid [[Army/Legends|Army]]. During the space battle, her [[Starfighter/Legends|starfighter]] was caught in a [[Tractor beam/Legends|tractor beam]]. As she called for assistance, she was pulled into range of the control ship's [[Turbolaser/Legends|guns]], which knocked out her fighter's [[Engine/Legends|engines]]. Palmer's ship was then shot down; however she was able to crash land successfully on the planet's surface. She radioed an update on her status to Lt. Sykes.\n\n==Personality and traits==\nAs a [[Pilot/Legends|pilot]] flying for the people of Naboo, Palmer was capable of piloting a number of craft ranging from the Flash speeder to the N-1 starfighter. She was also capable of leading a squad of Flash speeders into battle.
