While Rogue Squadron launched a [[Raid on Sullust|retaliatory raid]] on a [[Capacitor (Sullust)|Capacitor]] on the planet [[Sullust/Legends|Sullust]], Seerdon [[Attack on Thyferra|invaded]] Thyferra and took control of the planet's bacta factories.
