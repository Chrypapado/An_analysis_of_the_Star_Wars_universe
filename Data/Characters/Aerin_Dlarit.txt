For her efforts, Isard was installed as the world's [[Head of State/Legends|Head of State]] and [[Chief Operating Officer]] of the Xucphra Corporation.<ref name=\"Krytos\">''[[X-Wing: The Krytos Trap]]''</ref>\n\nUnder her rule, one of Isard's first actions was to establish the [[Thyferran Home Defense Corps]], a military force made up of Xucphran personnel to fight the [[Ashern]], a resistance movement led by the planet's original inhabitants, the [[Vratix/Legends|Vratix]].<ref name=\"Bacta\" /> Dlarit was among the high-ranking Xucphran officials who joined the Defense Corps, receiving the [[Military rank/Legends|rank]] of [[General/Legends|general]] despite having no military expertise.<ref name=\"TCSWE: Dlarit, Aerin\" /> In his daily duties with the Defense Force, Dlarit had to defer to [[Major/Legends|Major]] [[Barst Roite]]. However, Dlarit made up for his military shortcomings by attending several social functions and assuring the general public that the Ashern threat was being taken care of.<ref name=\"Bacta\" />\n\n===Raid on the Dlarit estate===\n{{Quote|Get the holocam up here. He dies a monument to his own ego and misplaced trust in the Empire.|Elscol Loro|X-Wing: The Bacta War}}\nIn the same [[Standard year/Legends|year]],<ref name=\"NEC\" /> Dlarit was selected as a target for [[Assassin/Legends|assassination]] by [[Elscol Loro]], a resistance fighter experienced in toppling planetary governments held by the [[Galactic Empire/Legends|Galactic Empire]]. With her were former [[Corellian Security Force|CorSec]] investigator [[Iella Wessiri Antilles|Iella Wessiri]], former Imperial [[Special Intelligence]] [[operative]] [[Sixtus Quin]], and Quin's [[Trooper/Legends|troopers]]. When Wessiri objected that Dlarit was hardly a notable military target, Loro countered that as a public figure, Dlarit's [[Death/Legends|death]] would be integral in frightening the planet's populace, thereby forcing them to choose between Isard's forces and the Ashern.<ref name=\"Bacta\" />\n\n[[File:Elscol.jpg|thumb|left|250px|Elscol Loro, who led the raid on Dlarit's estate]]\nOn the [[Local day/Legends|night]] Loro's [[Ashern strike team|strike team]] [[Raid on the Dlarit estate|infiltrated]] his [[Dlarit estate|estate]], Dlarit fell [[Sleep/Legends|asleep]] on his office chair while gazing at a [[Hologram/Legends|hologram]] of a statue that bore his likeness, surrounded by a group of adoring admirers and well-wishers. The strike team found him in that state and Loro was about to shoot him with her [[Blaster/Legends|blaster]] when Wessiri intervened. Proposing her own plan, Wessiri managed to convince Loro that rather than kill Dlarit, they could humiliate him instead, an action that would still serve their original goal of destabilizing Isard's regime.<ref name=\"Bacta\" />\n\nOblivious to how he almost died, Dlarit was [[Stun/Legends|stunned]] with a blaster shot from Wessiri. The strike team then stripped him of his [[Thyferran Home Defense Corps uniform]] and took a hologram of their deed, which they circulated throughout Thyferra and reaching as far as the [[Victory-class Star Destroyer/Legends|''Victory''-class Star Destroyer]] [[Corrupter/Legends|''Corrupter'']], which at that [[Time/Legends|time]] was en route to the [[Colonist/Legends|colony]] world of [[Halanit]] to punish the inhabitants for accepting free bacta from [[Wedge Antilles/Legends|Wedge Antilles]], the leader of the coalition of [[Pilot/Legends|pilots]] opposing Isard. Aboard the ''Corrupter'', Dlarit's daughter Erisi was mortified when she saw his image slumped naked on his chair.
