She was known for her skills as a [[Sniper/Legends|markswoman]], and was one of many criminals who frequented [[Jabba's Palace/Legends|the palace]] of the [[Hutt/Legends|Hutt]] [[Crime lord/Legends|crime lord]] [[Jabba Desilijic Tiure/Legends|Jabba Desilijic Tiure]] on [[Tatooine/Legends|Tatooine]]. Laudica was present in the palace in [[4 ABY/Legends|4 ABY]] when the [[Droid/Legends|droids]] [[C-3PO/Legends|C-3PO]] and [[R2-D2/Legends|R2-D2]] [[Siege of Jabba's Palace|arrived]] in order to bargain for the life of [[Luke Skywalker/Legends|their master's]] friend [[Han Solo/Legends|Han Solo]]. She witnessed several more of Solo's friends subsequently arrive until Jabba sentenced them to die in Tatooine's [[Great Pit of Carkoon/Legends|Great Pit of Carkoon]].\n\n==Biography==\nThe [[Human/Legends|Human]] [[Gender/Legends|female]] Laudica was born on [[Corellia/Legends|Corellia]]<ref name=\"db\" /> at some point before [[4 ABY/Legends|4 ABY]].<ref name=\"NEC\">''[[The New Essential Chronology]]''</ref> During the [[Galactic Civil War/Legends|Galactic Civil War]], she was employed as a [[Smuggler/Legends|gun-runner]] and had developed a reputation for her skills as a [[Sniper/Legends|markswoman]].<ref name=\"ccg\">{{CCG|set=Jabba's Palace Limited|link=JabbasPalace-Light/large/laudica.gif|cardname=Laudica}}</ref> Laudica was good friends with many other female criminals,<ref name=\"btm\">''[[Star Wars: Behind the Magic]]''</ref> including<ref name=\"ccg\" /> fellow [[Corellian/Legends|Corellian]] smuggler<ref name=\"Brindy DB\">{{DB|character|brindytruchong|Truchong, Brindy|archivedate=20100918105107}}</ref> [[Brindy Truchong]] and the<ref name=\"ccg\" /> [[Kiffar/Legends|Kiffar]] [[Con artist/Legends|con artist]]<ref name=\"Tonnikas' DB\">{{DB|character|tonnikasisters|Tonnika, Brea & Senni|archivedate=20110913184426}}</ref> [[Tonnika sisters/Legends|Tonnika sisters]]. Despite her better judgment, she was romantically involved with the [[Bimm (Near-Human)|Bimm]] [[Rayc Ryjerd]].<ref name=\"ccg\" />\n\n[[File:Laudica ROTJ.jpg|250px|thumb|left|Laudica watches Princess Leia bargain with Jabba.]]\nDuring the war, Laudica became a frequent visitor of [[Jabba's Palace/Legends|the palace]] of<ref name=\"db\" /> the [[Hutt/Legends|Hutt]] [[Crime lord/Legends|crime lord]]<ref name=\"CSWE\">{{CSWECite|II|130|Jabba the Hutt (Jabba Desilijic Tiure)}}</ref> [[Jabba Desilijic Tiure/Legends|Jabba Desilijic Tiure]]<ref name=\"db\" /> on [[Tatooine/Legends|Tatooine]]. She was in the palace in<ref name=\"rotj\" /> 4 [[Galactic Standard Calendar|ABY]]<ref name=\"NEC\" /> when [[C-3PO/Legends|C-3PO]] and [[R2-D2/Legends|R2-D2]], [[Droid/Legends|droids]] belonging to the [[Jedi/Legends|Jedi]] [[Luke Skywalker/Legends|Luke Skywalker]], [[Siege of Jabba's Palace|arrived]] in order to bargain for the life of [[Han Solo/Legends|Han Solo]], a friend of Skywalker's who was frozen in [[Carbonite/Legends|carbonite]] and who hung on the wall of the palace's courtroom. Shortly after the droids' arrival, Laudica watched as Jabba dropped his [[Slavery/Legends|slave]] dancer [[Oola/Legends|Oola]] into the pit of [[Jabba's rancor|his rancor]].<ref name=\"rotj\" />\n\nLaudica was in the courtroom shortly thereafter when Solo's friend [[Leia Organa Solo|Leia Organa]], disguised as the [[Bounty hunter/Legends|bounty hunter]] [[Boushh/Legends|Boushh]], arrived with the [[Wookiee/Legends|Wookiee]] [[Chewbacca/Legends|Chewbacca]] and threatened to destroy the palace with a [[Thermal detonator/Legends|thermal detonator]]. Organa stayed her hand and thus won Jabba's favor, but she later [[Rescue of Han Solo/Legends|freed]] Solo from carbonite and attempted to escape the palace with him. When Jabba caught and [[Prisoner/Legends|imprisoned]] them, Skywalker came to the palace to demand their release, and was dropped into the [[Rancor/Legends|rancor]] pit himself. He [[Death/Legends|killed]] the rancor, however, prompting Jabba to sentence him, Solo, and Chewbacca to die in Tatooine's [[Great Pit of Carkoon/Legends|Great Pit of Carkoon]]. Laudica and many other members of Jabba's entourage followed Skywalker and Solo as they were dragged out of the courtroom.<ref name=\"rotj\" />\n\n==Personality and traits==\nLaudica was a skilled markswoman<ref name=\"ccg\" /> and often carried a [[Blaster pistol/Legends|blaster pistol]].<ref name=\"db\" /> She had many friends,<ref name=\"btm\" /> and followed her romantic desires rather than her better judgment by dating Rayc Ryjerd.<ref name=\"ccg\" /> When Oola was dropped into Jabba's rancor pit, Laudica watched and jeered in delight as she was eaten by the creature.<ref name=\"rotj\" />\n\n==Behind the scenes==\n{{Quote|Mara fans that have painstakingly freeze-framed all the palace footage have found a number of hopeful contenders: the redheaded dancing girl that flirts with Boba Fett, for instance.
