Following an Imperial [[Destruction of Tondatha|attack]] on the [[Agamarian]] town of [[Tondatha]], [[Mon Mothma/Legends|Mon Mothma]], [[Chief of State (Alliance)|Chief of State]] of the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance to Restore the Republic]], went to Agamar in the hopes of bringing the resistance into the growing Rebellion. Subsequent to Mothma's speech to resistance members at a rally in the Agamarian [[Capital/Legends|capital]] of [[Calna Muun]], Poussan was one of four Agamarians who decided to enlist as a [[Rebel pilot/Legends|pilot]] in the [[Alliance Starfighter Corps]].<ref name=\"XWOSG\" />\n\nPoussan traveled to the waiting [[MC80 Home One type Star Cruiser|Mon Calamari Star Cruiser]] [[Independence/Legends|''Independence'']] aboard a Rebel [[Shuttle/Legends|shuttle]] along with the other new recruits, [[Keyan Farlander/Legends|Keyan Farlander]], [[Sunnar Jan-lo]], and [[Breth Gart]]. Upon arrival, the recruits were shown to their quarters and assigned liaison [[Officer/Legends|officers]] for the duration of their training. After learning the basics of [[Starfighter/Legends|starfighter]] operations in the [[The Maze (pilot proving ground)|pilot proving grounds]], the group proceeded to [[Holosimulator|simulated]] [[Starfighter combat/Legends|combat]] scenarios.
