Drevan was impressed by Niai Fieso's friendly demeanor and how it was used to swindle others, but he later attacked him from behind in a crowded arena<ref name=\"db\" /> after Fieso's betrayal cost him both his business<ref name=\"ccg\" /> and his credits.<ref name=\"cswe\" />\n\n==Behind the scenes==\n{{Quote|Freon was created for a CCG card game. While writing an entry on Niai Fieso<ref name=\"blog\">{{Blog|exar_xan|13|Behind the story - Niai Fieso|The chronicles of a writer|Sander de Lange|archivedate=20090808024154}}</ref>&mdash;a Xexto food vendor seen in the extended Podracing sequence on ''The Phantom Menace''{{'s}} [[Star Wars home video releases#Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace|DVD release]]<ref name=\"DVD\" />&mdash;de Lange chose Drevan over the Xexto [[Bounty hunter/Legends|bounty hunter]] [[Tosinqas]] while seeking an existing character of Fieso's species to work into his biography.<ref name=\"blog\" />\n\nBoth Drevan and Fieso were present at the [[Boonta Eve Classic/Legends|Boonta Eve Podrace]], and de Lange accordingly saw story potential in the connection; as Fieso had no existing biography, de Lange [[retcon]]ned him into being Drevan's former business partner. He intentionally left an open-ended conclusion to the two characters' stories in order to allow for future expansion by other authors.
