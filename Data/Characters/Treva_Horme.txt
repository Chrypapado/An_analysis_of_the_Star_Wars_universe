Ozz vowed to investigate but was interrupted in [[3 ABY/Legends|3 ABY]] when the [[Galactic Empire/Legends|Galactic Empire]] arrived and [[Occupation of Bespin|took control]] of the city. The partners decided to download all of their files and evacuate, but while Horme and Wiorkettle followed the plan, Ozz disappeared.
