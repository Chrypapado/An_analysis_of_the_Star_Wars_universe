For his acts of selfless valor, Hollis was awarded the [[Kenobi Medallion]] and a promotion.\n\n==Biography==\n{{Quote|We have spotted Imperial walkers!|Rey\u00e9 Hollis|url=http://www.wizards.com/swtcg/images/esb/rebel_hoth_army.jpg}}\nRey\u00e9 Hollis was born and raised on the<ref name=\"Who's Who\" /> [[Mid Rim]]<ref name=\"Essential Atlas\">''[[The Essential Atlas]]''</ref> [[Planet/Legends|planet]] of [[Naboo/Legends|Naboo]], the son of [[Lutin Hollis/Legends|Lutin Hollis]], who served with the [[Royal Naboo Security Forces/Legends|Royal Naboo Security Forces]] before and during the [[Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]]. Following in [[Gender/Legends|his]] [[Parent/Legends|father's]] footsteps, Hollis fought with the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance to Restore the Republic]] during the subsequent [[Galactic Civil War/Legends|Galactic Civil War]], eventually being assigned to the [[Alliance High Command/Legends|Alliance High Command]] headquarters of [[Echo Base/Legends|Echo Base]] on the [[Water/Legends|ice]] planet [[Hoth/Legends|Hoth]]<ref name=\"Who's Who\" /> by [[3 ABY/Legends|3 ABY]].<ref name=\"NEC\">''[[The New Essential Chronology]]'' dates the [[Battle of Hoth/Legends|Battle of Hoth]] to [[3 ABY/Legends|3 ABY]].</ref> At some point during his career with the Rebel Alliance, Hollis earned the [[Kalidor Crescent/Legends|Kalidor Crescent]], a prestigious award.<ref name=\"Who's Who\" />\n\nWhile on Hoth, Hollis served with Echo Base's complement of [[Alliance Special Forces/Legends|Special Forces]] [[Troop/Legends|troops]] under the command of [[Colonel/Legends|Colonel]] [[Ledick Firest/Legends|Ledick Firest]], as one of Firest's top [[Sergeant/Legends|sergeants]].<ref name=\"Who's Who\" /> When the [[Galactic Empire/Legends|Galactic Empire]] discovered the existence of Echo Base<ref name=\"ESB\" /> in 3 [[Galactic Standard Calendar|ABY]],<ref name=\"NEC\" /> Hollis helped prepare the base's perimeter defenses in the face of the imminent Imperial invasion.<ref name=\"ESB\" />\n\nHollis fought in the Rebels' icy trenches during the [[Battle of Hoth/Legends|Battle of Hoth]], warning his men to stay low to avoid being shot down by the Imperials' [[E-Web heavy repeating blaster]]s.<ref name=\"NEGWT\">''[[The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology]]''</ref> He distinguished himself during the battle by rescuing a cut-off [[Squad/Legends|squad]] of Rebel [[Soldier/Legends|soldiers]], pulling two of his medical personnel out of the way of an advancing [[All Terrain Scout Transport/Legends|All Terrain Scout Transport]], and finally being wounded while manning a [[DF.9]] anti-[[Infantry/Legends|infantry]] battery to cover the retreat of his [[Company/Legends|company]].<ref name=\"Who's Who\" />\n\nHollis was the only sergeant in Firest's five companies to survive the staggering Rebel defeat at the Battle of Hoth. He received the [[Kenobi Medallion]], a bronze device for his previously earned Kalidor Crescent, and a promotion for his acts of heroism during the battle.<ref name=\"Who's Who\" />\n\n==Personality and traits==\nHollis' Kenobi Medallion, earned during the Battle of Hoth, demonstrated his acts of selfless valor in helping to rescue several Echo Base soldiers, including his fleeing company, at the expense of his own safety. A [[Human/Legends|Human]] male,<ref name=\"Who's Who\" /> Hollis had light skin.<ref name=\"ESB\" />\n\n==Equipment==\nHollis wore the Rebel Echo Base field troops' standard [[Alliance Cold Weather armor|cold-weather armor]] during the Battle of Hoth, including [[Alliance Cold Weather Headgear|headgear]], [[Anti-glare goggles/Legends|anti-glare goggles]], [[Scarf/Legends|scarf]], [[Alliance Cold Weather Padded Vest|padded vest]], [[thermal flak jacket]], [[Alliance Cold Weather Gloves|gloves]], and [[Alliance Cold Weather Backpack/Legends|backpack]].
